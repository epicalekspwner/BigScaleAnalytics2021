Are you struggling like Corgi to select a suitable text for your level in French?

Check out our new tool below to analyze the difficulty of French sentences.

Sentence Difficulty Analyzer

About This Project

This tool was produced by Team Amazon as part of the group project of the Big-Scale Analytics course.

It aims to provide a model to help English speakers in their learning process by predicting the difficulty of a French sentence.

The difficulty is classified in the 6 levels defined by the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR): A1, A2, B1, B2, C1 and C2.
